Introduction

Soccer is one of the most popular sport in the world. From matches between international teams, to high tempo matches at club level, soccer is heavily viewed, enjoyed, and even celebrated in vast regions of the world. Players like Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo are even preached as Gods of football. This is because of the massive fan following that comes with this sport, and the level to which it has evolved. Teams spend millions of dollars to secure the best talents, improve their squad, the training and matchday facilities, etc. European Soccer is especially famous for its high level of competitiveness, which has led to high money influx in this sport in the form of club acquisitions, high transfer fees, stadium developments, endorsements, etc. Today, we aim to get some insights on the transfer values of some of the amazing soccer talents in the world.
